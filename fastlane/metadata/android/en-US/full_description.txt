<p>A lightweight bubble notice tool, providing more intuitive notifications for the Multitasking 'Bubbles' feature in Android 17, and is also applicable to lower Android versions that support "Bubbles", no "Root/Shizuku" required.</p>

<p><b>Features</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Subscribe to your favorite apps</li>
<li>Show persistent message bubbles for incoming notifications</li>
<li>Aggregate unread messages in a unified bubble console</li>
<li>Directly jump to specific chat pages when tapping notifications or unread cards</li>
<li>Dismiss bubbles to avoid distractions</li>
</ul>

<p><b>How to use</b></p>
<ol>
<li>Allow reading "Notification Access"</li>
<li>Manage Favorite Apps</li>
<li>Enable and display bubble</li>
</ol>

<p>After setting up BubbleNotice, when new notifications come, tap the bubble notification or the unread message card in the console to jump directly to the specific chat!</p>
